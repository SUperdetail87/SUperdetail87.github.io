About Me

Born on March 13th 1987, I am a young black man who enjoys great company and spending time with family! I am originally from Haiti. I immigrated here, to the U.S., in 2007 to reunite with my parents, looking for a better life. I enjoy playing basketball, soccer, videogames and I love tinkering with electronics; mostly computer builds! I enjoy coding in HTML, CSS and Javascript, from time to time, I do code in C++ but never really got the full hang of it. I enjoy doing research on aboutanything worth my time and this year my focus is back on coding.

I joined the US ARMY, New Jersey Army National Guard, in 2008 served 6 years, and was medically discharge in 2014. I graduated from Essex County College in 2015, with an A.S. Degree in Computer Information System. I am happily married to my lovely wife Laura! I am curently working at Crane Aerospace and Electronics as a Lead Plating-Coordinator Technician. With hopes for a better future working in the Information Technology field, I am now furthering my education by attending NJIT, where my major is B.S. in Management Information System with a minor in Mobile and Web Developement.
